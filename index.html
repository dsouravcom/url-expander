Frequently Asked Questions

What is a URL expander?

A URL expander is a tool that reveals the original, full-length URL behind shortened links (like bit.ly, t.co, goo.gl, etc.). This helps you see where a link actually goes before you click on it, protecting you from potentially dangerous websites.

Is this service really free?

Yes! Our URL expander is completely free to use with no hidden costs. We don't show ads or sell your data, making it one of the most privacy-focused URL expansion tools available.

How does this protect me from suspicious links?

By expanding short URLs, you can see the actual destination before visiting. This helps avoid phishing sites, malware, or other dangerous web pages hidden behind shortened links. Many cybercriminals use URL shorteners to disguise malicious websites.

Do you store the URLs I expand?

No. We don't store or log any of the URLs you expand. Your privacy is important to us, which is why we designed this tool to process URLs without keeping any records.

Which URL shorteners do you support?

We support all major URL shortening services including bit.ly, t.co (Twitter), goo.gl (Google), ow.ly, tinyurl.com, cutt.ly, rebrand.ly, and many others. If you find a shortener we don't support, please let us know!
